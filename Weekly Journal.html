Weekly Journal

Week of April 1st

This week we started our unit 1 CS CMU academy final task. The unit consisted only of how to create scenes and drawing shapes. I made mine a ORANGE guitar amp as I play guitar. I learned how to make smaller shapes and precise details in CMU. It took a long time but I like how it turned out.

Week of April 8th

This week we worked more on CMU. Getting through both Unit 1 and 2. I learned a lot about functions and events. It helped me come up with an idea for the next unit task.

Week of April 15th

This week we worked on CMU CS academy unit 2. Our final projects are coming to end. Mine is a volcano that you can erupt by dragging a slider. It took a long time to get the background to change with the slider and making the scenery using ovals and irregular polygons. I learned a lot about creating code in CMU more specifically how to drwa bushes, palm trees, etc.

Week of April 22th

This week we started our hardware unit. We worked on deconstructing a desktop computer and then proceeded to start on hardware projects. I learned about all the different parts of a computer along with the tedious work required to build them.

Week of April 29th

This week we finished up our hardware projects after working on them for the past month. The project consisted of building our own computer based on a profession. I chose to be gamer and make myself a supercomputer. My final budget was around $10 000. I've learned that if I want to make my dream computer I will have to work a job year round to start saving up money.

Week of May 6th

This week we kept working on our hardware posters and build your own computer projects. I was already finished so I worked on an additional game for CMU on unit 3. I started testing an idea for a hunter style game like in the arcades with the guns. I learned how to make a timer and make the concept and fundamentals of that game.

Week of May 13th

This week we started working on K8. K8 is a robot that we built that week and uses microbits to operate. The microbits can be programmed on the computer then downloaded onto physical bits. I learned about how to program and use a microbit and getting used to using K8.

Week of May 20th

This week we continued to work K8 with our groups. We drew our wall and prepared for the K8 showdown next week. We gave our K8 a pencil and drew some papers to tape to our robot which we named Cordelius. I learned how to drive the robot and make it go in all directions.

Week of May 27th

This week we cleaned up our websites and did our K8 battle. I learned that maybe we should have used the servos motor during the battle as our pin kept falling off during the battles. The website also got a background added which make it look a lot better.
